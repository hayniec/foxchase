The Community

Where Nature Meets Neighborhood

Hidden amidst towering pines and hardwoods, Foxchase feels worlds away, yet is just minutes from downtown Opelika and Auburn University.

At the heart of our community lies the pristine 60-acre Emerald Lake. Whether you're casting a line at dawn, paddling through the morning mist, or simply enjoying the view from your porch, life here moves at a gentler pace.

Emerald Lake

The Jewel of Our Community

Emerald Lake is a private, 60-acre lake managed exclusively for Foxchase residents. It serves as the stunning centerpiece of our neighborhood, providing year-round beauty and recreation.

From sunrise fishing to summer skiing, the lake is the heartbeat of life in Foxchase.
