Lake Life

A Private Oasis

Emerald Lake is a private, 60-acre lake managed exclusively for the enjoyment of Foxchase residents. It serves as the stunning centerpiece of our neighborhood, providing year-round beauty and recreation.

Whether you are taking a sunset cruise on a pontoon, teaching a grandchild to fish, or simply enjoying the view with your morning coffee, the lake offers a peaceful retreat from the busy world.

Lake Guidelines

To preserve the beauty and safety of Emerald Lake, we ask all residents to observe the following rules.

🚤

Boating & Skiing

Enjoy boating, kayaking, and water skiing on the lake. Lake Committee membership required for powered craft.

🎣

Fishing

Our lake is stocked with Bass and Bream. We encourage catch-and-release to maintain a healthy population for everyone.

🏊

Safety

Swimming is at your own risk. Life jackets are recommended for all boaters, especially children.
