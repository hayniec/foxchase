The Community

Where Nature Meets Neighborhood

Hidden amidst towering pines and hardwoods, Foxchase feels worlds away, yet is just minutes from downtown Opelika and Auburn University.

At the heart of our community lies the pristine 60-acre Emerald Lake. Whether you're casting a line at dawn, paddling through the mist, or simply enjoying the view from your porch, life here moves at a gentler pace.
