Our Story

More than a neighborhood. A community.

Foxchase has been a cherished part of Fredericksburg for nearly four decades. Tucked behind a canopy of oaks and maples, our streets are lined with character homes, mature landscaping, and the kind of neighborly warmth that's hard to find.

Our Homeowners Association works to maintain property values, preserve green spaces, and organize the events and programs that bring us together — from the annual chili cook-off to the holiday light contest.

Maintained Common Areas

HOA dues fund landscaping, trail upkeep, and facility improvements.

Active Committees

Social, architectural, environmental, and safety committees — all volunteer-run.

Transparent Governance

Open meetings, published budgets, and an elected board accountable to residents.

2026 HOA Dues

Annual dues are $480/year ($40/month), due January 15th. Payment can be made via the resident portal, check, or automatic bank draft.
